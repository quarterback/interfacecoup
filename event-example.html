Design For The Public Conference

Event Details

Date: October 15, 2024

Venue: Wacom Experience Center / Revolution Hall, Portland, OR

Type: Conference / Exhibition

About This Event

This event brought together designers, researchers, and public sector practitioners to explore how design thinking can improve government services and civic experiences.

The conference featured keynote presentations, interactive workshops, and an exhibition showcasing projects that demonstrate the impact of human-centered design in public spaces.

Attendees included UX researchers, service designers, policy makers, and technology leaders from across the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

Keynote Presentation

The keynote explored how consequence design principles can be applied to improve public service delivery.

The presentation covered key themes including service design in government, the importance of considering downstream effects of design decisions, and practical approaches for embedding human-centered design in policy development.
