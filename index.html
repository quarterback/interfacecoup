Unraveling complexity

I spent the past few months organizing the Portland Digital Corps, an all-volunteer civic tech sprint that matched project teams with non-profits. I spent a long time prior working on varying web roles from front-end development, service design, and information architecture. My work spans various levels of government, non-profits & private sector firms. I believe that involving designers to work across the product lifecycle including strategy and leadership improves product outcomes.
