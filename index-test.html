Ron Bronson

I work on the mechanics of systems most people don't see. Mostly public, often messy. Design, infrastructure, maintenance, and the places where trust falls apart.

I’ve led design teams in government, run civic tech programs, coached high school tennis, and taught systems thinking in architecture schools. I’ve worked on AI tooling, public infrastructure, vendor hellscapes, and compliance theater. It’s all the same job, really.

Lately: Deterrence tooling for large language models, participatory infrastructure experiments, and building out the Portland Digital Corps. I also teach at the University of Michigan in the Urban Technology program.

Always interested in weird problems, broken workflows, speculative civics, and people who like making things better without pretending it's simple.
