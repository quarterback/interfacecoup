Appearances

Over the past decade, I’ve spoken at conferences, universities, and organizations around the world about service design, interaction systems, digital infrastructure, and the consequences of how we build technology.

My talks have been featured at events like An Event Apart, Confab, IXDA Oslo, UXR Toronto, and Pixel Up! Cape Town. I’ve also guest lectured at colleges and universities across North America, Europe, and Africa.

I'm currently an affiliate of Joint Frontiers, a critical design collective based in Helsinki, where I collaborate on research exploring future systems and civic technology.
